USER RESEARCH
My teammate and I along with our user researcher met with Sales Engineers to indentify what needs to be improved. We asked them to detail out the experiences our customers have with the current product and what features they need to help them perform better. We met over WebEx and discussed with Sales Engineers all over the world.
